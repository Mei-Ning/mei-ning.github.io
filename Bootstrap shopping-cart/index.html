The Ultimate Surfer

The Ultimate Surfer gathers some of the world's most compelling surfers to live and train together as they battle it out on the consistent and perfect waves created at the Surf Ranch in Lemoore, California. The hopes of the contestants are collectively focused on the winner's prize -- an opportunity to compete on the WSL Championship Tour, the pinnacle of professional surfing.

Gabriel Medina (BR) 2021 Rip Curl WSL Finals Jersey

4 Reviews

These limited-edition commemorative jerseys celebrate the first-ever Rip Curl WSL Finals and this special moment in surfing history. For the first time, the jerseys represent each title contender’s ranking with a distinctive color that they will wear in the water throughout the event. The jersey art was designed by local artist and surfer Jeff Lukasik. Highlighting elements from the thriving ecosystem at his home break of Lower Trestles and made from recycled materials, the five custom jerseys honor the site of the title-deciding event while doing their part to protect it.
